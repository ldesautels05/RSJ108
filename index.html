Liv DesAutels' Interview How-To

An Instruction Challenge for JOUR108

How to record and create an awesome interview to enhance your story!

Assembly time: 15 minutes
Awesome rating: 100% awesome

Have you ever wondered how to execute an effective and engaging interview? Well, look no further! With this tutorial, you will learn how to record and perform an interview to take your story to the next level.

  • Things You'll Need

  • An email draft, asking your figure for an interview
  • A recording device (Tape recorder, cell phone, computer)
  • Notepad
  • Computer
  • Interview questions and follow-up questions
  • Microphone (Optional)

  1. Find a central figure for your story and find a time that works to meet.

    Once you have done this, confirm whether or not your meeting will be in person or over the phone.

  2. Write out your interview questions and follow up questions.

    What is the most important information that you need for the main idea of your story? What are some potential answers to each question, and how can you respond to them?.

    Physically writing down your questions with pencil and paper can be helpful to visualize your goals for the interview.

  3. Gather and set up your recording equipment.

    Make sure you can hear your interviewee clearly.

  4. Ask your questions and thank them for their time.

    Remember to ask if they have anything else they would like to add at the end. This is where you will get the best information!

  5. Transcribe your interview and form your quotes.

    Make sure you dotn cut out any important information, or change what your interviewee said.
